About Shop Production

“We are on earth to take care of life. We are on earth to take care of each other.” As we are working towards sustainability, these are the aspects that we are mainly focusing:

1. Location

We are currently located in a small town called Keningau. Although some of the process may take part in few other places in Sabah but most products are packed and shipped from here. We believe that being sustainable means being local.

2. Resources

Most of the fabric remnants/vintages/deadstocks are carefully picked from the thrift store nearby. Designs are made according to the type of material we get, we only make what is suitable and wearable for that specific material. We believe that being sustainable starts with the material - getting to know the material, understanding how the material works and giving it a second life is how we respect and value it.

3. People

Our products are handmade by local tailors. Wages are paid according to the request made by tailors or anyone involves in the production, fair trade is our top priority.

4. Quantity

Overproduction is not for us. We only produce small quantities at a time, as our aim is to avoid waste and unused garments that pile up in a big stockroom. Each piece should fulfil its purpose: being worn by one of you.

5. Packaging

We carefully select our packaging solutions, avoiding plastic and using recycled paper for all our cards. Our tags are locally printed, strings are from leftovers and our recycled paper cards are made with love in our studio using papers we collected over the years.
